Game Rules

Rule-1

A cell with 2 or 3 live neighbors will remain alive.

Rule-2

A cell with fewer than 2 or more than 3 live neighbors will die.

Rule-3

* A dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes alive. * A dead cell with fewer or more than 3 live neighbors remains dead.
